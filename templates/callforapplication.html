About AIC-NITF

AIC-Nalanda Institute of Technology Foundation, Bhubaneswar is a Leading Business Incubator & Odishaâ€™s first Atal Incubation Centre supported by Atal Innovation Mission, NITI Aayog, and Govt. of India and recognized by Startup Odisha, Govt. of Odisha.

AIC-NITF nurtures and provides support to the startups in the initial and critical stages of a new startup venture through customized and focused incubation support, mentoring, marketing network & investors connect.

What AIC-NITF Offers:

  • Seed Fund of up to Rs.25 lakhs from Govt. Grants accessible by eligible startups
  • Furnished Office Space
  • Access to Meeting Room and Conference Room
  • Hosting an Event at the Conference Room
  • High speed internet facility
  • Experience-based training covering live examples by experts
  • Capacity Building Training by Experts & Networking Events
  • Use of Fabrication Lab for development of Prototype on Payment basis
  • Access to Angel Investors and Venture Capitalist Companies.
  • Market Connect Facilitation to startups.

Who Can Apply?

Entrepreneurs with a focus towards building innovative solutions in Agriculture, IoT, Healthcare, Education, Financial Inclusion, Environment & Clean Tech and Social Impact. Preference will be given to startups that are using emerging technologies like IoT, AI, Block-chain, Robotics and Agri Tech.

Startups are invited to apply for the incubation program if you have:

  • An Idea/Early Stage Startups/ Advanced Startups
  • Seeking for Mentor & Support Services
  • Looking for Investment Advisory
