Eligibility

Anantya - Women Entrepreneurship Awards 2021 is to recognise Women entrepreneurs who belongs to the state of Odisha or whose enterprise is situated in Odisha

The awards shall have following three categories:

  1. Excellence Awards:
    • The incorporation age of enterprise should be more than 10 years and with an annual turnover of more than 10 crores.
  2. Leadership Awards:
    • The incorporation age of enterprise should be more than 5 years and with an annual turnover of more than 1 crore.
  3. Rising Startup Awards:
    • Startups at any stage of operation are eligible to apply for the recognition.

There is no age and qualification based criteria for the applicants.
