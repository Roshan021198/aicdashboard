About

Odisha Corporate Foundation (OCF) is an apolitical, non-profit making, non-religious society dedicated to social service, keeping the development of Odisha in perspective. We strive to organize and undertake activities to promote the social, economic and educational advancement of Odisha and its people including natives and people of Odisha origin or associated with Odisha residing in India and abroad. OCF is a registered society born out of collective commitment of eminent industry veterans and experts from diverse industries & geography to bring Entrepreneurs and Industry Professionals of Odisha origin to a single platform with an objective to leverage the collective leadership of our fraternity to support a culture of entrepreneurship in the state and re-establish Odisha as a Global Enterprise Hub. OCF is probably building the world’s 1st Community Based Global Network that will create a 360° connectivity for entrepreneurs from 30 districts of Odisha to 28 states & 9 UTs in India with the global fraternity and vice versa in a global to local and local to global format.

Women Entrepreneurship Awards 2021

Anantya – Women Entrepreneurship Awards 2021 is part of OCF’s commitment and priority for bringing about a massive thrust on Women Entrepreneurship in Odisha and among the fraternity worldwide. An eminent jury panel of professionals and domain based experts of Odisha origin and global significance is constituted to enable the most credible selection process of awardees with due recognition to the most merit based and inspiring women entrepreneurs and startups of the state. The nominations from entrepreneurs and startups will start from 10th March, 2021 and end on 25th March, 2021.
